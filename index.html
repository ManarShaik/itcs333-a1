Class Activities

Art and Craft Activities

Engaging in hands-on craft activities helps children develop fine motor skills and encourages creativity through materials like paper, glue, and colors.

Outdoor Activities

Outdoor play allows kindergarteners to explore nature, enhance physical fitness, and develop social skills through games and cooperative activities.

Mathematics Activities

Fun math activities, such as counting games and shape sorting, foster early numeracy skills while making learning enjoyable and interactive.

Music and Movement

Playing music introduces children to rhythm and melody, enhancing auditory skills and fostering self-expression through singing and instrument play.
